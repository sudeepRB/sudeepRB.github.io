Storyline
There will be a two character, one robot and another a doll. Both runs through a battery. The whole Scenes happens in a single room. Room will be full of toys. Robot and doll faces each other. Doll blinks her eyes. Robot steps towards a doll. Here , the walkcycle of robot will be shown. As soon as robot get closer to her, with robotic voice he says,” Soul here !!Soul here!!” robot touches the doll. Doll again blink her eyes. She also raises her hands up and down. They play for a while. Suddenly a battery of doll gets down and she falls down. Her life gets off now. Robot now take his battery and insert it into doll. As soon as he puts battery into doll, he falls down, Robot will say,” No Backup No Backup”. Now doll open up her eyes and finds robot lying on the floor..
