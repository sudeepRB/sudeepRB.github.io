Overview
www.youngstarclub.com is the proposed football club website. The club is presently located at Pokhara, Nepal. Founded on 2010, The main aim of this club is to promote local players. They conduct closed training camp for players. They also provide diet facility to the players during training. Club organizes one big national level competition and other local level competitions annually. Besides football, they are equally active in social events like blood donation program, sanitation program, vocational training, etc. This site will be the brand of club to let the viewers know more information regarding the club, players and events that has happened, that will happen and that's going on right now.
